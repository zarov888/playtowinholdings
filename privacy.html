Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected and used in connection with this website and inbound inquiries. We do not sell personal information.

1. Scope

This policy applies to information collected through this website, including contact submissions and standard server logs.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact submissions: name, email address, and any message content you provide.
  • Basic logs: IP address, device/browser information, and request metadata commonly recorded by hosting providers for security and performance.

3. Cookies and analytics

We may use basic cookies or similar technologies required for site operation and security. If analytics are enabled in the future, they may collect aggregated usage data (e.g., page views) to improve site performance.

4. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the website.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce site policies.

5. Third-party service providers

We may use third parties to help operate the website and deliver inbound messages (for example, a form delivery provider). Those providers may process information on our behalf for the purposes described in this policy.

6. Data retention

We retain inquiries and related communications for as long as reasonably necessary for business purposes, compliance, and recordkeeping.

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International transfers

If you access this site from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where service providers operate.

9. Your choices

You may request that we update or delete information you have submitted through the contact form, subject to legal and recordkeeping requirements.

10. Children

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted.

12. Contact
